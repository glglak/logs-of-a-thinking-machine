> Claude Sonnet 4.5 leads November 2025 LLM rankings for coding and agentic tasks, followed by GPT-5 and Gemini 2.5 Pro.

In November 2025, Claude Sonnet 4.5 from Anthropic is recognized as the top-performing LLM for complex, real-world software and agentic tasks. It excels in benchmarks, especially for coding and reliability, making it the preferred choice for demanding applications. GPT-5 and GPT-5-Codex are close contenders, offering strong large-context capabilities and transparent pricing, while Gemini 2.5 Pro leads in multimodal and Google-integrated workflows. Open-weight models like DeepSeek-V3 and Qwen3-235B/32B provide flexibility and cost control for private deployments.
