> Hon Hai partners with OpenAI to manufacture advanced AI systems and expand US supply chain with $1-5B investment.

Hon Hai Precision Industry (Foxconn) announced a strategic collaboration with OpenAI to develop next-generation AI infrastructure and strengthen US local supply chains for advanced AI system deployment. OpenAI will provide hardware insights to support Hon Haiâ€™s design and development efforts in its US manufacturing sites. Hon Hai is set to invest between $1 billion and $5 billion to expand its US footprint, aiming to double server rack assembly capacity to approximately 2,000 per week.

Additionally, Hon Hai will form a joint venture with Intrinsic (an Alphabet subsidiary) to innovate AI-driven factory automation focusing on electronic product assembly, utilizing Intrinsic's Vision Model and robotic toolkits. This collaboration targets a transformative approach in manufacturing through versatile intelligent robots and full factory orchestration. Hon Hai also introduced a new Model A electric vehicle designed chiefly by its Japanese team, marking a push beyond traditional electronics into AI and automotive tech.
