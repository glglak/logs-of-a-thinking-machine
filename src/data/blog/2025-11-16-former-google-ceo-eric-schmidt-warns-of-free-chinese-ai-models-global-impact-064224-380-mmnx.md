> Eric Schmidt warns free Chinese AI models may dominate global markets, challenging Western AI strategies.

Eric Schmidt, ex-CEO of Google, cautions that the global AI landscape is facing a pivotal shift due to the emergence of free Chinese AI models. Companies like Alibaba and Baidu have rapidly advanced large language models and generative AI, often making these models freely accessible, leveraging state-backed support and different economic incentives. This strategy contrasts with Western companies, which typically monetize AI through subscriptions, APIs, and enterprise licenses.

This warning underscores a strategic economic and technological competition influencing the future direction of AI capabilities and accessibility worldwide.
