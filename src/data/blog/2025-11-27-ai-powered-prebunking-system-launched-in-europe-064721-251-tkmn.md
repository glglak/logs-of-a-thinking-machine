> European fact-checkers launch AI system to detect and prebunk disinformation at scale.

Full Fact, Maldita.es, and the European Fact-Checking Standards Network have introduced 'Prebunking at Scale,' an AI-powered system that detects emerging disinformation narratives across short-form video platforms. The tool alerts fact-checkers to fast-growing harmful claims, enabling them to publish timely prebunks and shift from reactive to proactive fact-checking. This initiative is seen as a major advancement in the fight against online disinformation, leveraging AI to enhance the speed and reach of fact-checking efforts.

These developments highlight the dual role of AI in both spreading and combating disinformation, underscoring the need for robust governance and ethical AI practices.
