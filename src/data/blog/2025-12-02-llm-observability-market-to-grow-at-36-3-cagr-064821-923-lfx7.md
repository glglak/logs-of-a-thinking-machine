> LLM observability platform market projected to grow rapidly, driven by demand for transparency and regulatory compliance.

The market for large language model (LLM) observability platforms is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate of 36.3% from 2025 to 2029. This surge is fueled by rising adoption of generative AI across industries, increasing regulatory scrutiny, and the need for organizations to monitor, optimize, and ensure the reliability of their AI systems. Key trends include real-time monitoring, anomaly detection, drift analysis, and enhanced data privacy and compliance features within LLM pipelines.
