> Google launches biggest AI hardware engineering center outside US in Taipei, boosting AI infrastructure and innovation.

Google has inaugurated its largest AI infrastructure hardware engineering center outside the United States, located in Taipei, Taiwan. This new multidisciplinary hub will house hundreds of engineers working to accelerate AI innovation and hardware development. This move reflects a broader trend among American tech giants to regionalize AI infrastructure development closer to manufacturing hubs, rather than concentrating all operations in Silicon Valley. Google's investment includes expanding its data center presence in Asia Pacific and laying international subsea cables, fortifying Taiwan as a critical link in global AI data flow.
