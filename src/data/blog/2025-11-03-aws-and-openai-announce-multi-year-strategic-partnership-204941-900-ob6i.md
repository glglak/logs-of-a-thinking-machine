> AWS and OpenAI partner for $38B, scaling OpenAI's compute with AWS infrastructure.

AWS and OpenAI have announced a multi-year strategic partnership, granting OpenAI immediate and increasing access to AWS's advanced infrastructure. The deal includes Amazon EC2 UltraServers with hundreds of thousands of chips and the ability to scale to tens of millions of CPUs, supporting OpenAI's generative AI workloads. This $38 billion commitment will enable OpenAI to rapidly expand its compute capacity while benefiting from AWS's price, performance, scale, and security.
