> FPT is AWS-certified for generative AI, growing AI expertise with plans to train 50K specialists by 2030.

Vietnam-based global IT company FPT announced it has achieved the AWS Generative AI Competency, marking its recognition as an AWS Partner equipped to accelerate generative AI adoption for enterprises worldwide. This certification highlights FPT's capabilities in implementing services, tools, and infrastructure essential for generative AI solutions. FPT embeds AI across all products and operations, supported by AI Factories powered by NVIDIA GPUs ranked among the top 40 fastest supercomputers globally.

FPT currently employs over 25,000 AI-proficient engineers and 1,000 highly skilled AI specialists. It plans to train 1,500 new AI experts by the end of 2025 and reach 50,000 AI specialists by 2030 through comprehensive education programs spanning all employee levels and FPT University. FPT maintains strong partnerships with global AI leaders like AWS, Microsoft, and NVIDIA, conducting labs and hackathons to create tailored solutions addressing industry-specific challenges. Its strategic focus encompasses digital, intelligence, and green transformations, reinforcing its role as a major AI-driven innovator in IT services.
