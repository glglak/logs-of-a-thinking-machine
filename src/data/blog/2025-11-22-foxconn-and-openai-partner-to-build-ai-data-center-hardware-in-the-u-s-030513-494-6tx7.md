> Foxconn teams with OpenAI to co-design AI data center hardware, securing specialized infrastructure for growing AI demands.

Taiwanese electronics giant Foxconn has entered a strategic partnership with OpenAI to co-design and build AI data center hardware in the United States. This collaboration grants OpenAI early access to evaluate and potentially purchase the new AI infrastructure systems. With AI model sizes and inference requirements rapidly increasing, OpenAI aims to secure predictable, specialized hardware supply, while Foxconn looks to expand beyond consumer electronics assembly. This partnership could help control costs and improve performance for one of the most compute-intensive AI companies worldwide. The move signals growing vertical integration in AI hardware supply as demand for compute capacity tightens.
